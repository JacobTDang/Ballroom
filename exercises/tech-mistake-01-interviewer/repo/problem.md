# A Technical Mistake With Consequences

This is a timed behavioral mock. The interviewer (tutor pane, M-2)
will ask you the question -- answer out loud in the chat as you would
in a real interview, and build your full STAR answer in `solution.md`.

Expect follow-ups: interviewers probe the specifics ("what did YOU
do?", "what was the actual result?"). Vague generalities get probed,
not accepted.

You have 30 minutes. Submit with M-q to reveal the grading rubric,
then ask the interviewer for a graded assessment.
