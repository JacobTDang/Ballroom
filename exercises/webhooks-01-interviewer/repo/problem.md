# Order Events: the Webhook Surface

Design the webhook system for an e-commerce platform: merchants
subscribe their HTTPS endpoints and receive order events reliably —
even when their endpoints are slow, down, or compromised.

That is the whole prompt — just like a real interview. Scoping the
event catalog, the delivery guarantee, and the scale numbers is your
job: ask the interviewer (tutor pane, M-2) your clarifying questions,
then work through your design in `solution.md`.

You have 45 minutes. When you're done, submit with M-q to reveal the
grading rubric, then ask the interviewer for a graded assessment.
