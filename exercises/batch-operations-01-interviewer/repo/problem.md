# Bulk Import for Contacts

A CRM's contacts API has the usual per-contact CRUD. Customers now
need to import thousands of contacts in one go. Design the batch
surface.

That is the whole prompt — just like a real interview. Deciding the
atomicity, the error reporting, the limits, and what happens when a
half-succeeded batch is retried is your job: ask the interviewer
(tutor pane, M-2) your clarifying questions, then work through your
design in `solution.md`.

You have 45 minutes. When you're done, submit with M-q to reveal the
grading rubric, then ask the interviewer for a graded assessment.
