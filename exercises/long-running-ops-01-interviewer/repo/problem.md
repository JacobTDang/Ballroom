# Transcode Video: Long-Running Operations

Design the API for a video platform's transcoding service. A transcode
takes anywhere from thirty seconds to twenty minutes.

That is the whole prompt — just like a real interview. Scoping the
client experience (how callers start work, track it, cancel it, and
collect results) is your job: ask the interviewer (tutor pane, M-2)
your clarifying questions, then work through your design in
`solution.md`.

You have 45 minutes. When you're done, submit with M-q to reveal the
grading rubric, then ask the interviewer for a graded assessment.
