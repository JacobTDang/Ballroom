# Two Admins, One Settings Page

An organization's settings are edited through your API by multiple
admins at once. Design the update surface so nobody's changes are
silently lost.

That is the whole prompt — just like a real interview. Naming the
failure mode, choosing the mechanism, and specifying the client's
recovery are your job: ask the interviewer (tutor pane, M-2) your
clarifying questions, then work through your design in `solution.md`.

You have 45 minutes. When you're done, submit with M-q to reveal the
grading rubric, then ask the interviewer for a graded assessment.
