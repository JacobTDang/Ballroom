# Paginate the Product Catalog

Design pagination for a List API over a 100-million-row product
catalog that changes constantly while clients walk it.

That is the whole prompt — just like a real interview. Scoping the
clients, the orderings, and the consistency story is your job: ask
the interviewer (tutor pane, M-2) your clarifying questions, then
write the design in `solution.md` — token format, contracts, and
failure modes, with real field names.

You have 45 minutes. When you're done, submit with M-q to reveal the
grading rubric, then ask the interviewer for a graded assessment.
