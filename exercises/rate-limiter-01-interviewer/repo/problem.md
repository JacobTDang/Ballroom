# Design a Distributed Rate Limiter

Design a rate limiter for a public API platform: enforce per-client request limits across a fleet of API servers.

That is the whole prompt — just like a real interview. Gathering
requirements, scoping use cases, and putting numbers on the load are
your job: ask the interviewer (tutor pane, M-2) your clarifying
questions, then work through your design in `solution.md`.

You have 45 minutes. When you're done, submit with M-q to reveal the
grading rubric, then ask the interviewer for a graded assessment.
