# Upload 10 GB, Resumably

Design the upload API for a media service whose clients send files up
to 10 GB over unreliable connections.

That is the whole prompt — just like a real interview. Deciding what
"reliable upload" must mean (sessions, resume, integrity, the
small-file path) is your job: ask the interviewer (tutor pane, M-2)
your clarifying questions, then work through your design in
`solution.md`.

You have 45 minutes. When you're done, submit with M-q to reveal the
grading rubric, then ask the interviewer for a graded assessment.
