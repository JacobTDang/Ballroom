# Charge Exactly Once: Idempotency Keys

Design the charge-creation API for a payments platform so that client
retries can never double-charge — including a retry after a timeout
where the client never saw the response.

That is the whole prompt — just like a real interview. Gathering
requirements, scoping the resource model, and pinning the retry
semantics are your job: ask the interviewer (tutor pane, M-2) your
clarifying questions, then work through your design in `solution.md`.

You have 45 minutes. When you're done, submit with M-q to reveal the
grading rubric, then ask the interviewer for a graded assessment.
