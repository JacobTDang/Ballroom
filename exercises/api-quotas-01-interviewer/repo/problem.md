# Rate Limits & Quotas for the Platform

Design rate limiting and quotas for a public API platform with free
and paid tiers — the dimensions, the algorithm, the exact 429
contract, and fairness across tenants.

That is the whole prompt — just like a real interview. Scoping the
tiers, the traffic numbers, and what counts as abuse is your job: ask
the interviewer (tutor pane, M-2) your clarifying questions, then work
through your design in `solution.md`.

You have 45 minutes. When you're done, submit with M-q to reveal the
grading rubric, then ask the interviewer for a graded assessment.
