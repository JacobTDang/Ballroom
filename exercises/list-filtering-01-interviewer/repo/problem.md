# Filter and Order the Fleet

Design filtering, ordering, and search for the List endpoint of a
device-management API with two million devices.

That is the whole prompt — just like a real interview. Deciding which
fields filter, what the grammar looks like, what sorting promises,
and what your indexes can actually serve is your job: ask the
interviewer (tutor pane, M-2) your clarifying questions, then write
the design in `solution.md` with concrete syntax and examples.

You have 45 minutes. When you're done, submit with M-q to reveal the
grading rubric, then ask the interviewer for a graded assessment.
